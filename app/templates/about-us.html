About Us

Agriculture is the lifeblood of our nation. Without our farmers and their yield, we have no source of sustenance. This revelation, coupled with the problems wrought by the pandemic, was the impetus that led our team of eight on this path. Despite their obvious importance, our farmers are stuck using techniques and processes that lay far behind what they deserve. Our hope is that this website allows farmers to use the knowledge and wisdom of modern technology to supplement their plans with sufficient reasoning and foresight, while being able to find meaningful solutions to the problems that ail their existing crop.
